# Recent Advancements in AI Applications in Biomaterials and Cell Culture Optimization

## Introduction

The field of biomaterials and cell culture optimization has witnessed significant advancements with the integration of Artificial Intelligence (AI). This document provides an overview of recent developments, highlighting key methodologies, challenges, and future directions.

## Overview of Recent Advancements

1. Artificial intelligence-assisted next-generation biomaterials: From design to optimization. Recent advances in AI-driven protein engineering relevant to materials science are summarized, followed by an analysis of AI's role in designing, predicting, and optimizing next-generation biomaterials.
2. How artificial intelligence shapes the future of biomaterials: AI plays a crucial role in biomaterials design and development, enabling the creation of therapeutic or diagnostic interventions by interfacing with living systems.
3. Leveraging 3D cell culture and AI technologies for next-generation biomaterials: The integration of AI technologies and recent advancements in bioengineering optimize 3D cell cultures for biomedical research.
4. Artificial intelligence–assisted design, synthesis and analysis of smart biomaterials: AI influences the design and optimization of biomaterials, such as polymers used as extracellular matrices (ECM) for stem cell culture.
5. The Role of Artificial Intelligence in Biomaterials Science: A Review: AI models enable data analysis, pattern recognition, and property prediction in biomaterials science, highlighting key results achieved through AI applications in polymers for biomedical applications and advanced biomaterials.
