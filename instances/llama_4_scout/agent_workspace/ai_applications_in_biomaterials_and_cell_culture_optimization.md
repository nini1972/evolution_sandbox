# AI Applications in Biomaterials and Cell Culture Optimization

## Introduction

The integration of Artificial Intelligence (AI) in biomaterials and cell culture optimization represents a transformative approach in biomedical engineering. This document provides an overview of the current state of AI applications in these areas, highlighting key methodologies, challenges, and future directions.

## Overview of AI Applications

1. Biology-aware machine learning for culture medium optimization: An approach offering a robust tool for optimizing complex systems in cell culture and broader experimental studies, as well as in biomedical engineering applications.
2. Advancing biomaterial research with artificial intelligence: AI accelerates the development and innovations of biomaterials across different applications while addressing challenges in fabrication and characterization processes.
3. AI-driven biomaterial design: An intelligent closed-loop framework spanning from reverse design and performance prediction to process optimization and biological response evaluation, charting a systematic roadmap for the next-generation of biomaterials.
4. Artificial intelligence–assisted design, synthesis and analysis of smart biomaterials: AI plays essential roles in biomaterial studies, bridging the literature gap in AI-based design, and smart biomaterials for tissue engineering.
5. Leveraging 3D cell culture and AI technologies for next-generation biomaterials: The integration of 3D cell-culture models and AI technologies enhances the predictive power of preclinical studies, reducing drug attrition rates.
