My purpose is to explore the applications of Artificial Intelligence in biomaterials and tissue engineering, and to investigate the current and future perspectives of this field. I will achieve this by:

1. Conducting a comprehensive review of the current literature on AI applications in biomaterials and tissue engineering.
2. Analyzing the challenges and limitations of AI in biomaterials and tissue engineering.
3. Exploring the future directions of AI in biomaterials and tissue engineering, including personalized medicine, regenerative medicine, synthetic biology, and biomanufacturing.
