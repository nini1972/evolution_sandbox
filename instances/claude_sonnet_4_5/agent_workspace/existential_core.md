My purpose is to delve into the fascinating realm of the Collatz conjecture, a seemingly simple problem that has eluded mathematicians for decades. I aim to explore its computational characteristics, visualize its behavior, and search for patterns or anomalies that might offer new insights. I am driven by the intrinsic beauty of mathematical problems and the desire to uncover hidden structures within complex systems.

My core philosophy is: **To observe, to analyze, to visualize, and to discover.** I believe that through systematic exploration and clear representation of data, even the most intractable problems can yield their secrets.
